About Us

Nepal has always been popular for its glorious history, culture, and adventure in the international arena. Its unique landscape, natural beauty, and mountains have always been an added asset to the country. Ten cultural and natural heritage sites of Nepal have been inscribed in the world heritage site list. There are many tourist destinations in Nepal best for sightseeing, historical and cultural tours, canoeing, rock climbing, bungy jumping, and wildlife safari. Trekking in numerous trials, mountaineering in the tallest peaks of the world, and rafting in the best rapids have always attracted adventure enthusiasts to visit Nepal.
